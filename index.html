Bali
Digital
Heritage
Initiative

Empower Balinese communities in the preservation of the Balinese culture through
responsible, sustainable, value-based, innovative, and collaborative digitalisation efforts.

The BADHI project is a joint effort among Australian and Indonesian academics who believe that current digital technology presents a valuable opportunity to enhance the preservation of Balinese cultural heritage and to utilise the cultural assets for social good.
