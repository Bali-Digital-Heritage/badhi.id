About

The BADHI project is a joint effort among Australian and Indonesian academics who believe that current and future digital technology offers opportunities to revolutionise the preservation and utilisation of Bali's cultural heritage for social good.

Projects

BADHI's projects focus on bringing positive impacts to Balinese. Our projects encompass both tangible and intangible aspects of Balinese culture. Our current focus is on the preservation of temples and lontar manuscripts. More information about our projects will be available soon.
